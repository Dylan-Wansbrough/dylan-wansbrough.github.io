Blood Edge

Creating a Hack and slash game. (Group project).

When: Q2 2019

Where: Victoria University of Wellington

Programs used: 3D-Coat, Maya2018, Unity 5.6.3p1

Skills: 3D modelling, Level design, lighting, Game development and many more

About the project

This was a group project creating our own games in a group of 4-6. Unlike in last years course all of us were designers this time and so we had to split ourselves into different roles. I was a enviroemnt and UI artist but sadly we didnt finish up in time. As you can tell from the images we still were working on everything from UI to Environment design. This ultimately happened due to having too large a scope.

My roles: Level Design, Textures, UI, 3D Environment, Concept Art), Basic Programmer (Trigger events), Sound Design, Tester.
