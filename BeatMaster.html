Beat Master

Creating an arcade style game. (Project).

When: Q1 2019

Where: Victoria University of Wellington

Programs used: Unity 2018.2.1f1, PhotoShop CC

Skills: Game development, Scripting and Photoshop.

About the project

I do not own the rights to either of the songs used and the timing of the notes is based off of other peoples work.

For this project we were required to make a arcade game. This was was to help us work on programming in C# and better understanding what Unity has to offer. For my idea I picked a rhythm game, which had some issues along the way. The main issue was timing, everything had to be near perfect. If the timing was off the player would easily tell. The second part was feedback, making the player feel like they had hit a note. I spent a lot of time working on these aspects to make sure the game felt responsive.
