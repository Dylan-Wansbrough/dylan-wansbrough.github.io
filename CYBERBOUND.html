CYBERBOUND

Creating a cyberpunk first person shooter. (Group project).

When: Q3 2018

Where: Victoria University of Wellington

Programs used: 3D-Coat, Maya2018, Unity 2018.2.1f1

Skills: 3D modelling, Level design, lighting, Game development and many more

About the project

This was a group project creating our own games ina group of 6. 4 programmers and 2 designers. I was a designer but sadly the other designer didn't really contribute to the group. So I was left with most of the roles while he was Animation, Player model and Enemy model. This ment that the overall quality of everything else had to be sacraficed. Rest to be filled.

My roles: Designer, Artist (Textures, 3D Models, UI, 3D Environment, Concept Art), Basic Programmer (Trigger events), Level designer, Sound Design, Writer, Tester.
