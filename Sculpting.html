Sculpting Project

This was a project at University based around learning to 3D sculpt organic models using programs such as 3D-Coat or Z-Brush.

When: Q2 2018

Where: Victoria University of Wellington

Programs used: 3D-Coat, Maya2018

Skills: Sculpting, materials, texture painting, PBR, lighting, rendering, composition, rigging

About the project

Over the course of 4 weeks we had to design a character that will express a number of poses, each capturing a moment in time using a variety of angles in order to highlight the shape and material qualities of the character. This involved using sculpting tools such as 3D-Coat for the first time. It was quite a hard project, especially as we had to learn sculpting and 3D-Coat as we progressed through the course. We had to create 3 poses that represent our character and show off their personality.

In the 4 weeks I learnt a lot of new skills and had to overcome many new challenges. The character is rigged including finger ringing, although the face was not rigged but manually changed. Sadly I didn't get to put as much effort as I would have liked into the settings but due to time I had to make something quick. Im currently working on redesigning this model as I wasnt happy with it. Im currently starting with the anatomy. The outfit, Hair and face will be completely redesigned, probably with a cyberpunk theme.
