Interview - Mountain biking

Creating a interview about mountain biking in wellington. (Project).

When: Q2 2019

Where: Victoria University of Wellington

Programs used: Premiere Pro

Skills: Film/Photography and Video Editing.

About the project

For this project we were required to pick a hobby or job and interview people about it. I picked mountain biking due to wellingtons multiple moutnain biking tracks. This film required me to really focus on my photography and editing skills. Making sure that compisitions and shots were able to portray mountain biking in the best light.

This project was one of my first times editing film as such there is still room to improve and in the future I'd like to really play around and learn how to correct the video to make it look the best possible.
